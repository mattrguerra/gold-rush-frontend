Quick Answers

Common questions we receive

How does mobile detailing work?

We bring all equipment, water, and supplies to your location. All we need is access to your vehicle and a power outlet for some services. You can be at home, work, or anywhere convenient.

How long does a detail take?

Depends on the service: Basic wash takes about 45 minutes, interior detail is 2 hours, full detail is 4 hours, and ceramic coating can take 6-8 hours.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Richmond, Katy, Rosenberg, Sugar Land, Missouri City, Fulshear, Cinco Ranch, and surrounding Greater Houston areas.

How do I pay?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, Venmo, and Zelle. Payment is due upon completion of service.

What if it rains?

We monitor the weather closely. If rain is expected, we'll reach out to reschedule at no charge. Interior details can often still be performed in covered areas.

Do you offer recurring service?

Yes! Many customers book monthly or bi-weekly maintenance washes. Ask about our recurring customer discounts.
