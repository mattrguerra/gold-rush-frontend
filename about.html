About Gold Rush Detailing

Passion for perfection, one vehicle at a time

Our Story

From Car Enthusiast to Detailing Pro

Gold Rush Detailing was born from a simple passion: the love of a perfectly detailed car. What started as weekend projects on my own vehicles quickly grew into something bigger when friends, family, and neighbors started asking for help with theirs.

I realized that most people wanted their cars to look amazing but didn't have the time, tools, or expertise to make it happen. That's when Gold Rush Detailing was born—a mobile service that brings professional-grade detailing directly to you.

Today, we've detailed hundreds of vehicles across Richmond, Katy, and the Greater Houston area. Every car we touch gets the same attention to detail I'd give my own.

Why Mobile?

Detailing That Comes to You

Traditional detail shops require you to drop off your car, arrange a ride, and work around their schedule. We flipped that model.

Our fully-equipped mobile setup brings everything needed for a professional detail right to your driveway. You save time, skip the hassle, and your car never leaves your sight.

  • No waiting rooms or wasted time
  • Work from home while we detail
  • Watch the process if you'd like
  • Same professional results

Our Commitment to You

Every vehicle we detail is treated like our own. We're not satisfied until you are. If something isn't right, we'll make it right—no questions asked. That's the Gold Rush guarantee.
